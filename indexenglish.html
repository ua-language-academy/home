Who We Are

UA Language Academy, held in Kiev, provide foreign students with an intensive educational process for learning the Ukrainian language classes are aimed at speaking skills, listening, reading and writing. Attention is drawn to pronunciation, grammar, and lexical components. The entire training course is aimed at accelerating the process of integration of refugees and asylum seekers in Ukraine, overcoming the language barrier, the ability to explain their own needs in everyday situations and government institutions.

Program Description

level 1

The aim of this level is the learning of sound structure of language, reading and writing skills, elementary grammatical notions. Mastering communicative skills at entry level: ability to provide personal information: specify phone number, address, nationality; greetings, farewells, consents and disagreements.

level 2

The aim of this level is to study basic grammatical topics, improve reading and writing skills. To improve listening comprehension. Develop speaking skills (how to start a conversation yourself, to be able to ask questions and build a response), to expand vocabulary: acquaintance, daily situations â€“ in a shop (products), public transport, orientation in space.

level 3

The aim of this level is the mastering of the basic grammatical themes, the improvement of the skills of speaking, writing, reading and listening comprehension in situations of real life, such as talking over the phone, conducting household conversations, reading short articles from newspapers or magazines on the theme "Clothes", "Apartment", "Days of the week. Seasons "," My free time "," Rest ".

level 4

The aim of this level - mastering the basic grammatical concepts, the ability to build complex sentences; Improving listening and reading skills through analysis of short texts. Topics include medicine, receptions in government institutions. Development of communicative skills by constructing situational dialogues, discussion of problem issues.

level 5

The aim of this level is to learn to compare and analyze the received information. Discuss a given topic in groups in the form of discussion, oral story, appropriate use of grammatical structures. The topics of the discussion include information about the culture and traditions of Ukraine and their native country, travel.

level 6

The aim of this level is to be able to use the vocabulary and grammatical structures to express their views in different life situations clearly and coherently. To read and understand the written text. Take active part in formal and informal discussions on domestic, political and global topics.
